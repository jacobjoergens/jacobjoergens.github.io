What we do

Uncommissioned Design is a platform that connects people looking for custom furniture with a network of producers who can make it for them. Our mission, to democratize design, will look this:

  1. The network effects of a large community.
  2. A lower barrier-to-entry to the design industry.
  3. An easy, efficient and standardized way for everyone to get custom furniture.
