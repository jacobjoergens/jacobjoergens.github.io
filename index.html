what we do

Uncommissioned Design is a platform that connects people looking for custom furniture with a network of producers who can make it for them.

how we're doing

To date, Uncommissioned Design has been a passion project. We've been taking and making commissions for our friends (mostly fellow UChicago students for the past year. Our mission to make custom furniture more accessible for everyone has taken us on another journey. We are currently enrolled in the College New Ventu re Challenge and are actively researching, developing and testing our business model. You'll find a little slideshow of the projects we're the most proud of bel ow. If you're interested in getting a custom piece made or making a custom piece please scroll down to find our contact info and reach out! Before we put together the full-fledged platform we're workshopping the customer-producer interaction and would love to make as many connections and learn as much as poss ible.
